# Constitutional Interpretation & Non-Delegation Doctrine

## 1. Canonical Statement

All constitutional interpretation authority within the XC-Ecosystem is non-delegable and reserved to sealed constitutional governance mechanisms.

No automation, AI system, or subordinate policy may reinterpret constitutional law.

## 2. Non-Delegation Principle

Interpretation, amendment, or suspension of constitutional law may not be delegated to:

- AI systems  
- Platform administrators  
- External authorities  
- Policy engines  

## 3. Invalid Interpretations

Any non-canonical reinterpretation is null and void.

## 4. Sealed Enforcement

This doctrine is sealed, immutable, and retroactively binding across all constitutional domains.

Violation constitutes a constitutional breach.
