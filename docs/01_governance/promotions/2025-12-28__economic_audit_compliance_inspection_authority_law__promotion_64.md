# Economic Audit, Compliance & Inspection Authority Law

## 1. Canonical Statement

The XC-Ecosystem must maintain sovereign authority to audit, inspect, and verify all economic, billing, entitlement, and payment systems.

All financial operations are subject to constitutional inspection.

## 2. Audit Authority

Authorized inspectors may:

- Access all economic ledgers  
- Review settlement, reconciliation, and entitlement histories  
- Verify billing calculations and price authority  

## 3. Mandatory Compliance

All modules must provide inspection interfaces and non-obstructive access to auditors.

## 4. Disclosure Obligation

Detected discrepancies, violations, or anomalies must be disclosed and remediated.

## 5. Sealed Enforcement

This law is sealed, immutable, and retroactively binding across all economic and billing systems.

Violation constitutes a constitutional breach.
