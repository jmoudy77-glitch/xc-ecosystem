# Archive Law

All documents under /docs/99_archive are deprecated canonical contracts.

Rules:
1. Archived documents are permanently retired.
2. They may not be edited.
3. Every archived document must have:
   - Replacement path
   - Replacement version
   - Deprecation date
4. All deprecations must be recorded in the appropriate promotion ledger.
5. Archive is final; no document may be un-archived.
