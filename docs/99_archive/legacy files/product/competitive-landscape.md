# Competitive Landscape

## 1. Direct Comparisons

### MileSplit / Athletic.Net
- Strength: Massive results database.
- Weakness: Not a management system; limited team operations; outdated UI.
- XC‑Ecosystem advantage: Unified system + AI; eliminates middlemen dependency.

### SwimCloud (cross-sport analogy)
- Strength: Result database + simple profiles.
- Weakness: Limited team ops; not built to replace admin stack.
- XC‑Ecosystem advantage: Full operational suite + roster/scholarship builder.

### TeamBuildr / Volt
- Strength: Strength & conditioning.
- Weakness: No recruiting, no roster, no results system.
- XC‑Ecosystem advantage: Integrated S&C into full athletic context.

### ARMS / FrontRush / JumpForward
- Strength: Compliance and admin.
- Weakness: Not coach‑friendly; outdated UI; no practice or meet support; no AI.
- XC‑Ecosystem advantage: Coach‑first workflows with modern usability.

## 2. Why We Win Long‑Term
- Owning the athlete identity graph.
- AI as a decision engine for recruiting + training + roster planning.
- Universal meet + results system.
- Workflow unification: one login, one system, one record per athlete.
