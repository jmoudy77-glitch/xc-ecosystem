## 1. College Head Coach

## 2. College Assistant Coach
- Needs: Daily team ops, training assignments, communication, maintaining recruiting relationships.
- Pain points: Juggling tasks across tools, lack of visibility into program data.

## 3. High School Coach
- Needs: Roster and schedule management, practice planning, exposure for athletes.
- Pain points: No unified system, difficulty managing athlete development and college connections.

## 4. Athlete
- Needs: Transparent profile, results, training history, and recruiting visibility.
- Pain points: No control over their athletic identity across systems.

## 5. Meet Host / Event Manager
- Needs: Fast, reliable meet configuration, entries, seeding, and live results.
- Pain points: Old or unfriendly software, manual workflows.
