# Constitutional Amendment, Expansion & Canonical Evolution Law

## 1. Canonical Statement

All constitutional amendments, expansions, and canonical evolutions within the XC-Ecosystem must occur exclusively through the Promotion-through-Codex Protocol.

No informal, implicit, or undocumented modification is valid.

## 2. Amendment Exclusivity

The Promotion system is the sole lawful mechanism for:

- Constitutional amendments  
- Canonical expansions  
- Domain closure  
- Governance evolution  

## 3. Version Continuity

All Promotions are sequential, immutable, and permanently archived.

Constitutional history is non-erasable.

## 4. Retroactive Binding

All amendments are retroactively binding unless explicitly stated otherwise.

## 5. Sealed Enforcement

This law is sealed, immutable, and retroactively binding across all system domains.

Violation constitutes a constitutional breach.
