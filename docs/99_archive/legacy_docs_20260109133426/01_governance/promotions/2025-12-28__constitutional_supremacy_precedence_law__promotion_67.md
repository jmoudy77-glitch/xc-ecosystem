# Constitutional Supremacy & Precedence Law

## 1. Canonical Statement

The XC-Ecosystem Constitution is the supreme and final governing authority over all systems, modules, data, automations, economic constructs, AI operations, and human interactions.

No rule, contract, integration, feature, or subsystem may supersede constitutional law.

## 2. Precedence Hierarchy

In any conflict, the following precedence applies in descending order:

1. Sealed Constitution & Promotions  
2. Governance law  
3. Canonical doctrine  
4. Platform policy  
5. System configuration  
6. User preference  

## 3. Invalidity Clause

Any non-conforming rule, contract, or automation is null and void.

## 4. Sealed Enforcement

This law is sealed, immutable, and retroactively binding across all system domains.

Violation constitutes a constitutional breach.
