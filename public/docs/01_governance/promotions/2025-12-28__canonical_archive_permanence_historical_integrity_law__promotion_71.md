# Canonical Archive, Permanence & Historical Integrity Law

## 1. Canonical Statement

All constitutional artifacts, Promotions, governance laws, ledgers, and canonical records within the XC-Ecosystem are permanently preserved and historically immutable.

No canonical record may be altered, deleted, redacted, or suppressed.

## 2. Archive Permanence

Canonical archives must be:

- Write-once  
- Append-only  
- Tamper-immune  
- Permanently accessible  

## 3. Historical Integrity

All historical constitutional states remain permanently auditable.

No retroactive erasure is permitted.

## 4. Sealed Enforcement

This law is sealed, immutable, and retroactively binding across all system domains.

Violation constitutes a constitutional breach.
