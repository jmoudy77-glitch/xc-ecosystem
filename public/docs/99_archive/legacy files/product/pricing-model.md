# Pricing Model

## 1. Program Plans
- **Starter**
  - Basic roster management, limited recruiting board, basic practice planner.
- **Pro**
  - Full recruiting tools, team management, unlimited data, meet manager.
- **Elite**
  - AI features (commit probability, projection modeling), advanced analytics.

Billing stored in `program_subscriptions`.

## 2. Athlete Plans
- **Athlete Starter**
  - Manage own profile, verify performances.
- **Athlete Elite**
  - AI insights, advanced analytics, training history dashboards.

Billing stored in `athlete_subscriptions`.

## 3. Pricing Philosophy
- Value‑based pricing aligned with:
  - Time saved.
  - Competitive advantage.
  - Reduction of fragmented software spend.

## 4. Add‑Ons
- Meet Manager hosting fees.
- Data verification services.
